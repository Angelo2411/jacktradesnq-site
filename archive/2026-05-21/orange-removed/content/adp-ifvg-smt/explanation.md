### Why It Works

ADP is released the Wednesday before NFP and serves as a private-sector employment preview. The release moves index futures but with less violent post-release follow-through than NFP itself. NQ and ES are highly correlated, so requiring ES to confirm the mirror-side sweep filters out one-sided drifts that do not get broad participation.
