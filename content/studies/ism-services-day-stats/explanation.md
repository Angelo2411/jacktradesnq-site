## ISM Services PMI — Release Bar Range Stats

The ISM Services PMI is released by Institute for Supply Management at 10:00 ET. This study measures how much NQ futures travel high-to-low on the 1-minute release bar — i.e. the full top-to-bottom move during the 60 seconds of the release.

**Range 1m** = the high minus the low (in NQ points) of the release-time candle.
