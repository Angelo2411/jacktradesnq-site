## Model

Same engine as the NQ variant (IFVG inversion + opposite-leg sweep filter + "TP1 + BE" variant), executed on GC. Release time is 10:00 ET (Conference Board, last Tuesday of month); sweep window extends to 12:00 ET, resolve deadline 16:00 ET.

## SMT pair

NQ variants use ES as the SMT confirmation pair. GC variants use SI (Silver) — the closest correlated metals contract.

## Why This Event

Consumer confidence drives spending expectations, which feed into inflation and rate-path narratives — the primary driver of gold. A strong confidence print typically supports the dollar and pressures GC; a weak print does the opposite. SI tends to co-move when the sentiment read is clean.
