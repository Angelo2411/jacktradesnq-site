This study tests a reversal entry on Gold (GC) futures triggered by the Philadelphia Fed Index (Philly Fed) — a regional manufacturing survey released the third Thursday of each month at 8:30 ET. After the release sweeps a pre-news level, you wait for an Inverse Fair Value Gap (IFVG) to form, then enter when price breaks back inside the range, with a Silver (SI) SMT Divergence (SMT) confirmation filter. Tested across 10 years of GC data.

## Model

Same engine as the NQ variant (IFVG inversion + opposite-leg sweep filter + "TP1 + BE" variant), executed on GC. Release time is 08:30 ET (Philadelphia Fed, third Thursday of month); sweep window extends to 10:30 ET, resolve deadline 16:00 ET.

## SMT pair

NQ variants use ES as the SMT confirmation pair. GC variants use SI (Silver) — the closest correlated metals contract.

## Why This Event

Regional manufacturing data influences USD direction through rate-expectations. Philly Fed is an early signal ahead of national ISM Manufacturing — a strong beat can lift yields and pressure gold, while a miss does the reverse. SI confirms when the metals reaction is broad rather than isolated to GC.
