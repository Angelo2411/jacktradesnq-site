## Article 1 — ISM Manufacturing Points (GC)

The ISM Manufacturing is released at 10:00 ET. This study measures how much GC (Gold) futures travel high-to-low on the 1-minute release bar — the full top-to-bottom move during the 60 seconds of the release.

**Range 1m** = the high minus the low (in GC points / dollars per oz) of the release-time candle.
