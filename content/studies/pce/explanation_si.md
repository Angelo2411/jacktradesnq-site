## PCE Core Straddle — Silver

Silver straddles around the monthly PCE Core release at 8:30 ET. As the Fed's preferred inflation gauge, PCE determines the rate-path trajectory — and Silver, with its dual monetary/industrial identity, reprices on both channels simultaneously.

These datas were my own tests to see which stop/TP combos perform best on Silver futures around PCE. I remind you guys that these datas are gathered with AI and this is **NOT financial advice** — datas could be wrong, so backtest yourself.

### Setup

OCO buy-stop / sell-stop ±Offset from release close in SI pts, TP at fill ±TP, no SL, 30-min expiry. Bilateral straddle across Stop/TP combos tested over a 10-year window.

Silver's PCE reaction can be more volatile than Gold's — the dual-demand profile amplifies the move when both channels align. The backtest quantifies when that amplification is tradeable.
