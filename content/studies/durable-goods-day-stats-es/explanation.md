## Article 1 — Durable Goods Points (ES)

How far does the S&P 500 (ES) travel when Durable Goods Orders — the monthly report on long-lived goods manufacturing — hits at 8:30 ET? This tracks the high-to-low range of the 1-minute release candle on every print from 2016 to 2026.

**Range 1m** = the high minus the low (in ES points) of the release-time candle.
