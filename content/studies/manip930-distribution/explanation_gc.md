In 2025–26, after the 09:30–10:00 move on GC, the 10:00–11:00 hour continues that direction 49.4% of the time — indistinguishable from a coin flip. Over the full 2016–2026 sample (2,600 days), the continuation rate is 49.5%.

Gold shows no directional persistence between the two windows, in any regime. 2025: 48.2%, 2024: 51.0%, 2023: 46.7%. The rates oscillate around 50% with no discernible trend — exactly what you would expect from a random process.

On sweep days (1,183 of 2,600), continuation is 50.5% — also random. Gold's 24-hour trading cycle means the 09:30 open carries less overnight information than equity indices, which may contribute to the lack of directional persistence.

Direction is defined mechanically: sign of close−open for each window. This is an observational study, not a trading strategy. No entries, stops, or targets are implied.
