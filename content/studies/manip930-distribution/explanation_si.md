SI (silver futures) is the wildcard of the group. The 2026 regime delivers a striking PF 1.83 for Hold to 11:00 — but this comes with a massive caveat: silver's tick size is tiny. Net points of +6.5 sound insignificant until you recall SI is $5,000 per point, meaning that modest edge translates to approximately $32,500 in 2026 alone.

The strategy has been profitable in 2025 (PF 1.75, net +15.5 pts) and 2026 — a strong recent trend. However, the 10-year picture is more muted (PF 1.04 full period), and silver's notorious volatility means drawdowns can be sharp.

SI is the highest-upside play for this setup if you can stomach the whipsaw. The recent regime suggests the pattern is real and strengthening — but silver is not for the faint of heart.
