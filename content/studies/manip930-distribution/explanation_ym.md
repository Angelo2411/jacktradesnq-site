In 2024 (data through December 2024), after the 09:30–10:00 move on YM, the 10:00–11:00 hour continues that direction 53.0% of the time — a modest lean toward continuation. Over the full 2016–2024 sample (2,265 days), the continuation rate is 49.9%.

YM data ends at 2024-12-31 — no 2025–26 data is available, so we cannot assess whether the continuation lean observed in 2024 persists into the current regime. The 2024+ window (effectively just 2024, 253 days) shows 53.0% continuation, but this is a single year and fragile.

On sweep days (1,029 of 2,265), continuation is 52.5% — slightly above the full-sample rate but still within coin-flip range.

The Dow's large average move size can be misleading — it doesn't translate into directional predictability. Direction is defined mechanically: sign of close−open for each window. This is an observational study, not a trading strategy. No entries, stops, or targets are implied.
