In 2024 (data through December 2024), the 09:30 sweep-reject fires on ~50.2% of YM days. The 10:00 distribution follows through 49.2% of the time — a coin flip — with an average move of +1.0 points.

Over the full 2016–2024 sample (2,284 days), manipulation occurs on 49.3% of days with 51.5% follow-through. **YM data ends at 2024-12-31** — no 2025–26 data is available, so we cannot assess whether the pattern persists in the current regime. The 2024+ window (effectively just 2024) shows 49.2% follow-through and a near-zero average move.

Like other equity indices, YM's 09:30 manipulation is frequent but the 10:00 distribution has no directional edge. The Dow's large average favorable excursion (66.7 pts) can be misleading — it doesn't translate into closing follow-through.

This is an observational study of market microstructure, not a trading strategy. No entries, stops, or targets are implied.
