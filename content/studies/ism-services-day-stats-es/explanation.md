## Article 1 — ISM Services Points (ES)

The ISM Services is released at 10:00 ET. This study measures how much ES futures travel high-to-low on the 1-minute release bar — the full top-to-bottom move during the 60 seconds of the release.

**Range 1m** = the high minus the low (in ES points) of the release-time candle.
