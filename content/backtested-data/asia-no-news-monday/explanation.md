Backtesting results for trading NQ futures on Mondays during the Asian session when no major news events are scheduled.

The strategy filters out high-impact news days and focuses exclusively on the Asian killzone behavior during low-volatility Monday opens.

### Setup Rules

1. Only trade Mondays with no red/news folder events
2. Enter during the Asia killzone window (20:00–00:00 EST)
3. Target 1:2 risk/reward ratio
4. Maximum 1 trade per session

Preliminary results show a win rate above 60% over a 12-month sample period.
